Rules

Do not send nor include:

  • 1. Personal information.
  • 2. Spam or self promotion.

For some inspiration, you can:

  • - Make a confess.
  • - Tell stories.
  • - Tell white jokes.
  • - Tell your opinion about something.
  • - Talk about some topic.
  • - Ask anything to the creator of this website (me!).

Messages must be processed before they can be approved and answered. If your message does not meet the rules, it may be ignored, and your IP address may be banned from this site.
